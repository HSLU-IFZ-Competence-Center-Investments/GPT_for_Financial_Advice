Global presence

SHL has always been dedicated to a global outlook, transgressing language barriers and time zones. We combine the best talent, knowledge, and practices from all over the world to offer first-in-class design, manufacturing, assembly, and final assembly solutions for our clients globally.

Switzerland

Corporate headquarters, tooling and assembly equipment production for SHL Medical

China

Assembly equipment production for SHL Medical

Taiwan

Design, development, and manufacturing for SHL Medical and SHL Technologies

Sweden

Design and development of products for SHL Medical 

USA

Design, development, final assembly, packaging, and labeling for SHL Medical

Mexico

Assembly equipment production for SHL Medical

Switzerland

As part of our ongoing efforts to expand SHL’s footprint in Europe, we established the Zug HQ to be closer to our Continental customers and open new opportunities for pursuing sustainable operations and innovative technologies.

To help meet the demand for our market-leading products, the Zug site also has strong capabilities in design and process development.

Taiwan

As SHL’s primary production site, our Taiwan location boasts strong capabilities in the manufacturing, process development, and product care of our drug delivery systems.

With more than 4,900 employees in Taiwan, we take pride in the inclusive working environment established here and the consistent innovation and creativity that it delivers.

United States

Situated in Florida, our US site primarily focuses on final assembly, labeling, and packaging, offering pharma partners a turnkey solution for SHL-designed devices from development to market launch.

This vertically integrated development model enables a parallel development process that shortens timelines to fulfill customer requirements for Quick-to-Market™ services.
