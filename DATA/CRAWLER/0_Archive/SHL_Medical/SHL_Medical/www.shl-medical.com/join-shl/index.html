Innovation Pioneers Wanted:

Join SHL Today!

SHL is unique place to start an exciting career. An inclusive company, we value diversity in our workforce and the wealth of ideas it brings. The possibilities for success at SHL are endless, and your passion to drive change is the key.

View openings

Join SHL

At SHL Medical, our work is integral to the delivery of modern precision medicine. The company’s vitality springs from our people. Our medical and healthcare products make a real difference to people’s lives all over the world, and we value the staff who make this possible.

Read More

SHL invests continuously to pioneer in design, development, and manufacturing. Our sites unite R&D teams working on innovative products right at the frontier of medical science with high-quality precision manufacturing.

If you would like to be challenged, be rewarded, and feel valued, browse the sites below to find out about our stimulating careers around the world.

Why should you join SHL?

At SHL Medical, we are redefining careers. We provide the right tools for our employees to flourish in their careers. Join us and together we’ll shape the platform for your success.

Ownership & Impact

Experience a culture that encourages ownership and control over your outcomes, making you a key player in SHL’s success. Your work contributes to the development of products that significantly impact people’s lives.

Comfortable Working Environment

Break free from the norm and embrace a modern, dynamic workplace. From the factory floor to offices, our modern facilities are clean, air-conditioned environments that ensure comfort and well-being.

Proactive & Engaged Workforce

We encourage you to be proactive, engaged, and contribute your ideas. Shape your career and influence the success of the company. Join a team that thrives on collaboration and values every individual’s input.
