CAPABILITIES

SHL is a renowned, high-volume, high-quality manufacturer of drug delivery devices. We have developed in-house manufacturing capabilities to maintain better quality control and supervision over our materials and processes. This results in rapid response times in development as well as consistent quality in production, both on high-volume and/or low-volume/high-value projects.

To provide optimal manufacturing capacity for our customers, SHL has expanded its production facilities exponentially in Taiwan, where top pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies and other industry experts from around the world frequently conduct audits.

  • Tooling

    • State-of-the-art production equipment and processes
    • Multi-cavity hot runner technology
    • Extensive tool design and manufacturing experience
    Read More
    SHL’s comprehensive in-house tooling program guarantees the delivery of precise, reliable, and efficient production. We follow detailed production plans and strict quality measures to ensure the timely delivery of high-performance plastic injection molds. We invest in top quality equipment and best-in-class machining technologies to ensure the optimal efficacy and highest quality manufacturing.

  • Molding

    • High-precision molding machines
    • Multi-component molding
    • Fully-automated material handling system
    Read More

    Our wide selection of molding machines are equipped with high tonnage capabilities and advanced robotic technologies. We utilize state-of-the-art equipment and related software, and use the latest machining technologies to manufacture high-precision multi-cavity hot runner molds. All our machines are connected to fully computerized material handling systems to ensure the quality and specifications of the molded parts meet our high standards.

  • Automation

    • Semi- and fully-automatic testing/assembly machines
    • Integrated robotic systems
    • Comprehensive servicing and maintenance support
    Read More

    SHL designs, develops, and manufactures advanced testing and sophisticated assembly automation equipment that are fully compliant with international standards. Developed to ensure precise, consistent, and repeatable processes, our automation program helps customers increase output, reduce labor costs, and minimize potential component contamination.

  • Assembly

    • Customized assembly solutions
    • Robust manufacturing processes
    • Flexible ramp up capabilities
    Read More

    SHL work closely with our customers to develop assembly solutions that best suit our partners’ needs. We provide semi- and fully automatic high-speed assembly services using machines designed and custom-built by our in-house automation experts. This allows us to meet our customer’s forecasts as well as their requests for scalability, repeatability, and process control.

  • Metrology

    • Fully programmed metrology instruments
    • Automated measurement activities
    • Functional, environmental, and reliability verifications
    Read More

    SHL has set up multiple metrology laboratories equipped with high-performance measurement machines and state-of-the-art computed tomography (CT) scanners to conduct precise inspection of components and equipment. The instruments are fully programmed and automated to ensure all activities are accurate, repeatable, and reproducible.

  • CNC Machining

    • Advanced computer numerical control machinery
    • Quality manufacturing for high volume output
    • Carefully designed standard operating procedures
    Read More

    SHL invests in advanced, multifunctional CNC equipment to ensure maximum quality at high-volume outputs. Our manufacturing sites are capable of working on projects with high degrees of complexity. To safeguard the efficiency and quality of our services, our highly skilled professionals follow detailed standard operating procedures to maintain superior services focused on meeting customer demands.
