Fruits and vegetables

At Frigoveneta we know well the fruit and vegetable world: we come from a highly agricultural area and the first industrial systems that we made were for the preservation of nectarines, cauliflower, apples, radishes and melons: crops that are typically found in our area.

In addition to the fruit and vegetables preservation cold rooms, we are also specialized in controlled atmosphere cells for ripening fruit, cold rooms with special ventilation for small fruits.

We know that system reliability, energy savings and ease of maintenance are important factors for our customers because they allow you to recover resources and competitiveness: in order to achieve these results we have implemented remote control technology and the design of the entire system.
