NILT at Mobile World Congress 2024

Join us in Barcelona for MWC, the world’s largest and most influential connectivity event.

Visit our booth in the European Pavilion, Hall 8.1, Booth #8.1A20 

NIL Technology (NILT) is an optical solutions company designing, developing, and manufacturing optical elements and components based on Meta Optics. 

By using Meta Optics NILT can create next generation of optical modules that are smaller than existing solutions, contain fewer elements, are more thermally stable and can contain more functionalities.     

NILT will be showcasing:

Miniature cameras for Eye Tracking, 3D sensing, Driver Monitoring (DMS), and other applications where form factor and quality is important.

Illumination modules: dot projectors or flood projectors for 3D sensing or LiDAR applications

High quality masters for manufacturing of waveguides for AR.
