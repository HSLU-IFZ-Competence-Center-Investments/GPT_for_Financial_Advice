How to reach us:

From Linate Airport, Milan: Take either the eastern or western ring road around the city and then take motorway A9 toward Como/Chiasso. Exit at ‘Como Sud’, the first exit after the Como tolls gate.

From Malpensa Airport, Milan: Take motorway A8 toward Milan, and than motorway A9 toward Como/Chiasso. Exit at Como Sud, the first exit after the Como tolls gate.

From Orio al Serio Airport, Bergamo: Take motorway A4 toward Milan-Turin . After the Milano Est toll gate, exit at Cinisello Balsamo and take highway S36 toward Lecco. Take street S342 toward Como.

From Milan city: Take motorway A9 toward Como/Chiasso. Exit at Como Sud, the first exit after the Como tolls gate.

From Agno Airport, Lugano: Take the motorway toward Chiasso/Milan/Italy. Take the Como Sud exit.

From the COMO SUD EXIT: Follow the road signs for ERBA/LECCO or BERGAMO. Follow the road toward Lecco until you get to the Tavernerio junction, then follow the road S342 toward Bergamo.

From HIGHWAY S36: Follow the road signs for ERBA /LECCO until you get to the Lurago d’erba roundabout. Take a left turn toward Como.

From COMO SUD: After the Tavernerio junction go through one traffic light and then go on for about 1 km more; at the second junction, turn right, following the road signs indicating Airport/Verzago.
After about 200 m you will see the Previero- Sorema industrial complex on your right.

From HIGHWAY S36: After passing through the center of Alzate Brianza, go through round about, and when you get to the second junction turn left, following the road signs indicating Airport/Verzago.
After about 200m you will see the Previero-Sorema industrial complex on your right.

HOSPITALITY AND TOURIST INFORMATION

La società appartiene alla famiglia PREVIERO da 3 generazioni. All’inizio degli anni ’20, Natale Previero inizia la propria attività con la rappresentanza di macchine per l’industria tessile e del legno nel territorio di Como.
