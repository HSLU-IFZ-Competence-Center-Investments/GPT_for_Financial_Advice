These grinders are designed to grind pipes and profiles of various diameters with a length up to 6.5 m.

The automatic feeding with horizontal pusher allows also the recycling of blocks and other production scraps.

3 models available:
MU 100/80: for pipes up to Ø 600 mm
MU 120/100 TO: for pipes up to Ø 800 mm
MU 180/150 TO: for pipes up to Ø 1350 mm

The oleodynamic tilting of the cutting chamber ensures an extraordinary easy access and cleaning which cannot be compared with the traditional grinders.

Suitable for high productions and frequent color change and materials.

2 models available:
MU 420 AS: with Ø 400 x 2000 mm rotor
MU 816 AS: with Ø 800 x 1600 mm rotor

Innovative grinding concept with 2 rotors.

The big dimension of the cutting chamber ensures high performances with voluminous materials.

Available in 2 models:
DR 242: with cutting chamber: 800 x 1200 mm
DR 225: with cutting chamber: 1000 x 1500 mm

Available in 2 models:
PZ 502: with Ø 500x200mm rotor
PZ 506: with Ø 500x600mm
