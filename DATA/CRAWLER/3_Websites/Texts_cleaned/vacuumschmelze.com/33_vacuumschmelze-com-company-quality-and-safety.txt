The sustainable customer satisfaction and the resulting success of our company is achieved through our ability to meet the requirements and expectations of our customers and interested parties in the long term.

To achieve this goal we have made the strategic decision to introduce, maintain and continuously improve a quality management system.

On the basis of documented procedures and interfaces, we actively manage and control the interacting processes and resources in order to create the values required to achieve the desired results.

 Safety, Health, Environmental Protection and Energy
 "SAFETY FIRST, in everything we do!"

We assume special responsibility for our employees and attach importance to global standards in occupational safety and environmental protection at all our locations.In addition, the Hanau site complies with the requirements of DIN EN ISO 50001 (energy management system).

Furthermore we are in close contact with our representatives of the authorities in accordance with accident legislation. We communicate not only reportable but also non-reportable incidents openly with our contact persons. As a result of the intensive commitment of our employees, there has never been a major event in the long history of VAC.

In the context of implementing our strategic and operational goals, we are particularly committed to sustainability in production. In Consequence, we minimize the environmental impact and reduce the used resources equally.
