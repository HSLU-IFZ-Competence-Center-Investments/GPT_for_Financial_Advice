    Bed Bug Pest Control

    One of the most frightening things you can find in your home, bed bugs are a nuisance that will leave you curled up on the living room sofa for weeks. Often picked up during travel or on public transport, these nasty critters will invade any house or hotel they can find, regardless of cleanliness. A nearly impossible DIY pest, only a trained pest control expert will be able to completely and finally rid your property of these teeny tiny bugs once and for all. Using both chemical and heat treatments, the team at Flick Anticimex is proud to provide thorough treatment solutions and comprehensive educational resources that ensure both the proper removal of your infestation and the prevention of future invasions.

    Learn More about Bed Bugs

    Ant Control Solutions

    An all too common nuisance across Australia, ants are voracious eaters that will stop at nothing to hunt down food. Attracted by everything from picnics to pantries, only an expert pest control technician will know what it takes to rid your home or business of these six-legged critters once and for all. With a range of treatment solutions that both target ants in their nests and block their points of entry, our comprehensive range of services is appropriate for all sizes and species, from the common carpenter ant to the highly invasive fire ant.

    Learn More about Ant Control

    Bird Control Solutions

    While listening to the sound of birds singing outside your window may be a peaceful way to relax, having them settle around your home can lead to both ongoing mental frustration and irreparable structural damage. At Flick Anticimex, we are committed to long-lasting and cost-effective treatment solutions, with each plan designed specifically to the unique architecture and functionality of your property. From netting and spikes to complete shock systems, our state-of-the-art tools and technology ensure that your home or businesses remains free from invasive and irritating birds once and for all. Carriers of mites, parasites, and bacteria that can pose a health risk to your family and customers alike, even the most common of species has no place making a home of your hearth.

    Learn More about Bird Control

    Flea & Tick Control Solutions

    Pet lovers beware, a Fido-friendly home is often a harbinger of invasive pests. Prone to hitching a ride on dogs and cats alike, ticks and fleas will enter your home and soon overrun it, bringing with them not just discomfort but potentially serious health consequences. At Flick Anticimex, we are committed to both the integrity of your home and the safety of your family, and have developed unique treatment solutions to ensure complete tick and flea removal from your property. Experts in our field for over 100 years, we target these blood-thirsty bugs at all stages of life, ridding your pets and property of them once and for all.
