We are ensuring responsible business practices

A comprehensive approach to responsible business practices

At SHL Medical, our goal to forge a robust and sustainable business framework is firmly grounded in ethical practices.

By 2030

we will manifest a sustainable business foundation for our people and supply chain.

We empower our people and endorse ethical business principles to become an even more inclusive organization

We strive for a sustainable value chain by ensuring our suppliers to adhere to our ESG targets

By 2025

30%   Share of women in senior management roles 

100% Strategic suppliers signing Code of Conduct 

90%   Relevant suppliers covered by a sustainability assessment

100% Employees trained on ethics annually 

By 2030

<1      Lost Time Injuries per million working hours

>7.8   Employee Engagement score

Upholding ethical principles and ensuring sustainable value chains

CORPORATE ETHICS

Comprehensive ethical training programs are provided to employees, fostering a culture of responsible behavior and integrity across the organization.

HEALTH & SAFETY

We prioritize cultivating a workplace prioritizing employee health and safety, establishing an environment that promotes well-being while proactively preventing injuries.

EMPLOYEE RECRUITING & DEVELOPMENT

It is one of our core focus areas to identify top-tier talent and foster their growth through robust developmental initiatives, which empower our employees to excel, thereby elevating overall employee satisfaction.

DIVERSITY, INCLUSION & EQUAL OPPORTUNITY

We uphold the intrinsic worth of diversity, inclusion, and equal opportunity, fostering a culture where all individual’s unique perspectives and backgrounds are embraced and valued.

SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT

By 2025, a concerted effort is underway to ensure that all strategic suppliers are committed signatories to the Code of Conduct. Additionally, a comprehensive evaluation process involving ESG assessments and audits is targeted, reflecting our proactive approach to supplier engagement and ethical standards.

TRANSPARENCY, ACCOUNTABILITY & REPORTING

We believe that transparency is critical to building trust with our stakeholders and driving change. For this reason, we pursue the approach of proactive Reporting in line with evolving standards.
