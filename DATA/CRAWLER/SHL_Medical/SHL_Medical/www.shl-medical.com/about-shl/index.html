SHL at a glance

SHL Medical is a world-leading solutions provider in the design, development, and manufacturing of advanced delivery devices such as autoinjectors, pen injectors, and innovative specialty delivery systems for large-volume and high-viscosity formulations.

We also offer design-to-build and contract manufacturing services for products such as laboratory handling equipment, neurosurgical devices, and industrial equipment.

By integrating our experienced development teams with efficient manufacturing and a skilled workforce, we have developed a successful business model that allows us to provide comprehensive solutions for demanding requirements of the industry. 

Our Purpose - Enabling Patients' Independence

At SHL, we believe that people living with chronic conditions should be defined not by their diseases, but by the everyday experiences that shape the core of their identities. That is why we partner with pharma companies to develop advanced drug delivery systems that help enhance the self-injection experience. This is how we enable patients’ independence.

Our products make a real difference to people’s lives all over the world, and we value the people who make this possible.

Our Values

SHL is a purpose driven company and our values guides us in our operational and strategic work. Our values are:

  • Focus on Customer
  • Operate with Ethics and Integrity
  • Drive Simplicity
  • Learn and Improve
  • Deliver Together

Together with our strategy and our transformative agenda, they help us fulfil our company purpose.

OUR DEDICATION

INNOVATION

We have dedicated years of expertise, commitment, and resources to developing world-class healthcare solutions that empower people.

QUALITY

We believe in delivering quality, innovation, and leadership for all stakeholders in the industries we serve.

COMMITMENT

We will continue being pioneers in everything we do, with a focus on constant growth and improvement of our people, our services, and our potential.

GLOBAL PRESENCE

SHL has always been dedicated to a global outlook, transgressing language barriers and time zones. We combine the best talent, knowledge, and practices from all over the world to offer first-in-class design, manufacturing, assembly, and final assembly solutions for our clients globally.

Get to know SHL

SHL combines world-class manufacturing in Asia with global design visions and the strengths of Western management practices to offer our clients first in class products and services.

A strategic global presence allows us to maintain close contact with our customers while ensuring streamlined management and communication throughout the project timeline. Our design centers in Switzerland, Sweden, Taiwan, and the US focus on designing and developing innovative products, and our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Taiwan and the US utilize an ISO 13485-certified quality system to manufacture and assemble them.
