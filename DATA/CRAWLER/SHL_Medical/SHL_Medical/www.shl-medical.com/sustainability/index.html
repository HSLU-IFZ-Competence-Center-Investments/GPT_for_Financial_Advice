SUSTAINABILITY AT SHL MEDICAL

Taking bold action towards a sustainable future

At SHL Medical, our commitment to sustainability is deeply ingrained in every facet of our operations. Through our dedication to innovation and technological advancement, we enable patients’ independence and help people manage their health journeys. We hold steadfast in our pursuit of long-term sustainable business practices, recognizing that our responsibility extends beyond the immediate horizon. Through deliberate and strategic efforts, we champion initiatives that actively diminish our ecological footprint and create lasting value for society

Turning vision into action: Our Sustainability Strategy

Our Sustainability Strategy for 2030 encompasses a triad of dimensions, each further articulated into subdimensions. This structure culminates in a coherent framework of material topics that seamlessly pave the way for concrete objectives and action items completed until 2030.

WE ARE DRIVING PATIENT HEALTH AND INDEPENDENCE

By 2030, we will expand our portfolio with innovative and pioneering devices to improve patient outcomes and independence.

READ MORE

WE ARE REDUCING THE ECOLOGICAL FOOTPRINT

By 2030, we will significantly reduce GHG emissions, introducing circular principles and reduce waste impact.
READ MORE

WE ARE ENSURING RESPONSIBLE BUSINESS PRACTICES​

By 2030, we will manifest a sustainable business
foundation for our people and supply chain.

READ MORE

Leading the way: Our Confirmed Near-Term Targets 

The Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) has approved SHL Medical’s near-term science-based emissions reduction target. As part of our sustainability strategy,  

  • we commit to reducing absolute direct (Scope 1) and indirect (scope 2) greenhouse gas emissions by 42% by 2030 from a 2022 base year. 
  • we commit to reducing our indirect (scope 3) greenhouse gas emissions by at least 51.6% per Mio. units sold by 2030 from a 2022 base year.
