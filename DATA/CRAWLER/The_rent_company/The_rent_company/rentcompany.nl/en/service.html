Service

 

Something can always go wrong, but the lessons continue. That is why, in consultation with the school, we provide replacement equipment at the ICT desk or student secretariat. We solve the problem and in the meantime pupils and students can continue to learn as normal. During the holiday period, when schools are closed, we have a home service.
