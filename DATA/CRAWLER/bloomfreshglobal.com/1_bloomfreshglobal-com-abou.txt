We strive to create a better world – a fruit industry that is productive, accessible, global, and sustainable, where we can all enjoy healthy, surprising, and delicious experiences.

Our Mission We are the global leader of fruit innovation, using natural and non-GMO new breeding practices, for a healthier, tastier, and more sustainable future for growers and consumers worldwide.

We strive to be the world’s preferred fruit breeder and leader in agrotechnology. Nonconformity is one of our non-negotiable values. We will continue expanding our innovation in all our actions and products.  We believe that building a better, tastier and more sustainable world for all is in our hands.

The world would be a better place if everyone could…Have a more productive, more accessible, more global and more sustainable fruit industry with which we can all enjoy healthy, surprising and, of course, delicious experiences. ABOUT US
OUR VALUES 
Innovation The companies we have merged have been leaders and pioneers in natural breeding. Now our innovation will go even further.
Nonconformism Innovation is in our DNA and wouldn’t be possible without our unwavering commitment to continuous improvement and our relentless pursuit of quality in every action we undertake.
Sustainability We develop new resistant, productive, and adaptable varieties. This implies a reduction in the use of pesticides or the optimization of irrigation systems, among other benefits for the environment.

Our success is the success of our growers. We work with them transparently and closely, joining forces to empower them and achieve the best results together.
