About us

CF Partners is CFP Energy

As the energy world has evolved, so have we.

Following a period of tremendous growth and investment, we are pleased to announce CF Partners is becoming CFP Energy. The new brand and identity will create closer alignment across all group companies and reinforces our commitment to providing leading energy and environmental solutions to all our customers.

The transition to a low-carbon economy demands action from all parties. CFP Energy will continue to provide our global customer base with innovative solutions across environmental products, power markets, and alternative fuels.

For our climate and your business

Since 2006, we have been connecting you to the insight and opportunity you need to thrive in an ever-changing energy world.

How we help you

Bespoke service. Comprehensive solutions

We provide large and complex energy users with a single interface for all their energy supply and hedging needs. This includes direct access to energy markets, tailored environmental products and power management services.

Through our combined solutions, we enable you to accelerate towards your energy and climate goals, enhanced by our industry leading risk management expertise.

The road to decarbonisation

We recognise that decarbonisation cannot always happen overnight, so we work with you to establish a robust strategy to manage your energy transition journey.

James McRobbie, General Counsel 

James has over 15 years of post-qualification experience in law and finance and was responsible for developing the legal counsel function of the business. Mark Smith has over 25 years’ experience in accounting and finance across private equity and investment management. Before joining CFP Energy in 2014, Mark worked as a finance manager at a NBGI Private Equity and as an accountant at Gordian Knot, an investment management company.

Lee Evans, Director of Treasury & Regulatory Affairs 

Lee has almost two decades of experience in Treasury, across multiple products, managing exposures and trading Foreign Exchange (FX) and Interest Rate markets. In addition to income generation, Lee was the creator and owner of the 'Thought Leadership' business in Bank of Ireland, representing the Group on various external platforms including television, radio and print in addition to digital content creation. Lee is a Chartered Banker, a Certified Treasury Professional and is currently completing a course with the Institute of Directors in Ireland to become a Chartered Director.

Graham Stokes, Head of HR
