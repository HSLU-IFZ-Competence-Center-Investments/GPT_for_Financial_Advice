Refrigerating units and chillers

In FULLCO2 machines for transcritical-cycle systems (booster), the CO2 exchanges heat with the outside air and is thus forced to work above the critical point for most of the year (especially in hot climates). The main advantage in this case is that of having a system running entirely on CO2, a readily available “natural” gas that does not harm the environment. These machine guarantee zero greenhouse gas emissions. Frigoveneta technology applied to the FULLCO2 range guarantees the utmost operating safety combined with high energy performances.
