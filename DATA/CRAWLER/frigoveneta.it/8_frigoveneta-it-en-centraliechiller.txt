Our range of refrigerating units and chillers is designed for maximising efficiency and are manufactured with ISO 9001:2018-certified procedures. Thanks to an extensive assortment of power outputs, overall dimensions and refrigerant fluids, these products can satisfy the requirements of all food and non-food sectors regardless of the size and number of usage points to be cooled. Our mission is to offer lasting, high-performance products that respect the environment and ensure personal safety.

The entire production process takes place in-house at the headquarters based in Villa Bartolomea, where each machine is pre-cabled, tested and subjected to numerous quality and operation tests during specific phases. ach component used and every operator involved in the production process are traced to guarantee quality and reliability. We also design and manufacture the command and control electrical enclosures, which are likewise subjected to rigorous quality and testing procedures.
