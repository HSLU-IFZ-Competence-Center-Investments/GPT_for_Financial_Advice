Producer and Landowner Information

Genera’s wet lap market pulp is a high-quality, short fiber option for a variety of industries. These include towel, tissue, paper, and packaging board. Produced from agricultural biomass using a sustainable and environmentally-friendly process, Genera’s market pulp offers a domestic alternative to imported non-wood fiber options and hardwood fiber. A virgin fiber, Genera’s market pulp is suitable for food contact products. Our fiber is natural in color, offering customers a natural appearance and environmental benefits over bleached material.

For more information regarding market pulp, please contact sales@generainc.com.
