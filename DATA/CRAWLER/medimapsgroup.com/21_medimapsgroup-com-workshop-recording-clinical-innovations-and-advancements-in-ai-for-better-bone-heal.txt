Workshop Recording : Clinical Innovations and Advancements in AI for Better Bone Health

We were pleased to welcome you at ECR 2024 for 2 insightful workshop sessions ‘Clinical Innovations and Advancements in AI for Enhanced Bone Health’.

This workshop was the occasion to:

✅ Gain insights into the latest guidelines for the clinical application of TBS for fracture risk assessment and osteoporosis management.
✅ Learn more about the recent advancements in AI for osteoporosis management from classical machine learning to ​​more advanced models.
✅ Explore how AI can augment the overall clinical workflow from prevention to screening, treatment and monitoring of osteoporosis.

With Prof. Karen Hind, Ph.D., Director of Clinical Affairs at Medimaps Group & Guillaume Gatineau, Ph.D. candidate in Artificial Intelligence in the Interdisciplinary Center of Bone Diseases at Lausanne University Hospital and Data Scientist at Medimaps Group.
