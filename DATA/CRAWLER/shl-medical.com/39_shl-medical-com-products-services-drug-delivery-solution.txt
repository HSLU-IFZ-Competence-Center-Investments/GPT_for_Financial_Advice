Home / Products & Services / Drug Delivery Solutions 

SHL Medical is a world-leading solutions provider of advanced drug delivery systems, such as autoinjectors. Driven by our company purpose – Enabling Patients’ Independence, we partner with leading pharma and biotech companies to pioneer the development of patient-centric solutions that support the enhancement of patients’ quality of life through self-injection systems as well as digital healthcare solutions. With over 5,000 employees located across our Switzerland, USA, Sweden and Taiwan offices, we rely on our comprehensive in-house manufacturing capabilities to provide end-to-end services, from design, development to final assembly, labeling, and packaging. 

SHL Medical is a recognized leader in the design, development, and manufacturing of advanced drug delivery devices such as autoinjectors, pen injectors, and inhalation systems. We also provide a comprehensive range of solutions and services from preconfigured devices, bespoke project development, to final assembly, labeling, and packaging. 

Our customers include top pharmaceutical and biotech companies that require innovative devices to deliver their drugs effectively. These devices empower patients by allowing them to safely self-administer medications in the comfort of their homes.

Many of our devices have received several prestigious awards and demonstrated pioneering technologies that have shaped the self-injection industry. Our extensive offering includes disposable and reusable devices that can adapt to specified requirements through robust customization. Such specifications may include fixed or variable dosing, specific primary containers, high drug viscosities, connectivity, and more. 

Our passion for innovation is reflected in the number of device projects successfully brought to the hands of patients. Learn more about some of our most outstanding projects so far. 

SHL offers a comprehensive range of services to support the success of your drug-device development.
