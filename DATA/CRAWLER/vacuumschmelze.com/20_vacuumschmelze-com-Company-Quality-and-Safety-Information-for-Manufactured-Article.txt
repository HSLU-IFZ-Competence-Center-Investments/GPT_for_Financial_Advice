 Information Sheets for Manufactured Articles

The semi-finished and finished products manufactured at VAC are classified as 'articles' under the REACH Regulation (EC) No. 1907/2006. No legal obligation to produce and distribute a safety data sheet applies to products that are 'articles' as understood by REACH. The comprehensive information typically contained in a safety data sheets is summarized in the alloy-specific "Information sheets for articles". We expressly point out that these information sheets are voluntarily produced data sheets to which the requirements of the REACH Regulation do not apply. Information sheets are available in two languages (German/English). Information sheets for manufactured articles for the US market are available in English.
