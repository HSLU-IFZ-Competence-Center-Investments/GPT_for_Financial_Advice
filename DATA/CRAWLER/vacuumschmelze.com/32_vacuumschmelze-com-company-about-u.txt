 VACUUMSCHMELZE is a Leading Manufacturer of Magnetic Materials and Solutions

We passionately advance the technologies of today and tomorrow. As a reliable partner, we work with our customers to develop application solutions that make it possible to meet constantly increasing requirements. We push technical boundaries with groundbreaking solutions. The use of our materials as well as our refined solutions and their special magnetic properties are the key to making our customers’ systems smaller, lighter, more efficient and, last but not least, safer. Thereby we contribute significantly to the saving of resources and the protection of our environment.
